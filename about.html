about

I'm Jackie, a student at UC Berkeley, originally from Southern California. The phrase "eyes unclouded" is from one of my favorite movies, Princess Mononoke. I fell in love with the character Ashitaka, who strove to "see the world with eyes unclouded by hate." His way of living resonated with me, and now that's something I aim to do: to see a beautiful world unclouded by hate, and to hopefully project what I see onto this blog through my photography.
