I'm Jackie, a student at UC Berkeley, originally from Southern California.

I like to say that I inherited my love for photography from my father, who taught me everything I needed to know to spark my passion for it. I initially used photography as a way to document my travels around the world, but have since expanded more into portrait photography. I love focusing on people, their personalities, and their emotions, but I will always love a good adventure that focuses on the beauty of the world.

Feel free to contact me about anything! I love working with others and collaborating, and would be extremely happy to do so.
